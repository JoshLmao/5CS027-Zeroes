\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Spike actor -\/ Controls a spike for Daoko\textquotesingle{}s ability one, which will rise from the ground and disappear after a duration. Also deals damage to any \mbox{\hyperlink{class_a_enemy_base}{A\+Enemy\+Base}} it collides with.
